For Sparta!

You are a lanista in ancient Rome, attempting to raise the status of your ludis by winning gladiatorial competitions. To win competitions, you need a good balance of gladiators that are Strong, Dexterous, and Intelligent. Each competition will test a different combinations of skills.

The game takes place over 2 phases, each with 6 rounds. In phase 1, you bid on lots of slaves, each lot contains one slave per player. Winning a bid will grant you first choice of slave from that lot. If you drop out of a bid, you will get half (rounded down) of your money back and are still able to select a slave before players who have passed before you.

Once you have amassed 6 slaves, the games can begin (Phase 2). Each round in the second phase represents a competition. The competition type is listed at the top of the playing area, and it will dictate the character traits required to be successful this round (eg: Str * Dex indicates that your gladiator's strength will be multiplied by his dexterity. Largest number wins). Winning a competition grants you the largest prize from the pool, second best takes second-best prize and so on.

The lanista with the most money at the end of the two rounds is declared the winner!
